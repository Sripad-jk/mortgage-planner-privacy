Privacy Policy for Mortgage Planner

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

Mortgage Planner ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains our data practices.

No Data Collection

Our app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data:

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • We do not track user behavior or analytics
  • We do not store any user data on our servers
  • We do not share any information with third parties
  • All calculations are performed locally on your device
  • No account creation or login is required

Third-Party Services

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect certain information for advertising purposes. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Storage

All data entered into the app (loan amounts, interest rates, etc.) remains solely on your device. We have no access to this information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
